The Tools

Welcome

Welcome The New Employee: Sarabot introduces new employees and encourages them to introduce themselves with a fun question.

Conversation Starters

Topic Ideas and Open-Ended Prompts: Sarabot provides remote employees with topic ideas and open-ended prompts to help spark conversations with their coworkers.

Matching Users

Matches Users Based on Question Responses: Sarabot sends questions to public Slack channels and matches employees based on similar answers.

Team Building Resources

Suggests Games: Sarabot also provides some helpful team building resources like simple games you can play with two or more players.
